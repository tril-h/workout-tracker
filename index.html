Hypertrophy log

Warm up once. ~5 min general + 1–2 ramp sets on the first exercise of each push/pull/leg pattern. Not every machine.

Effort. Stop most sets with 1–3 reps in reserve. Last set of isolation/machine work can go to ~0–1.

Rest. 2–3 min on heavy compounds, 60–90s on isolation. Superset the lettered pairs (SS A/B/C) to save time.

Progress (double progression). Hit the top of the rep range on every set → add weight next time and drop to the bottom. The placeholder shows last session — beat reps or weight.

Rotation. Just do the next day each visit: Upper A → Lower A → Upper B → Lower B → repeat. 3 or 4 days/week both work.
